# Privacy Policy

 Last updated: 29 April 2026

 ## Overview

 Australian Citizenship Test Practice ("the App") is designed to help you prepare for the Australian Citizenship Test. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains
 how the App handles your data.

 ## Data Collection

 The App does not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or share any identifying details. The only data transmitted off your device is (1) text you
 choose to translate via the optional translation feature, and (2) anonymous subscription events sent to RevenueCat to verify your Premium entitlement, as described in the
 Subscriptions and Payments section below.

 ## Local Storage

 All app data — including quiz progress, test history, question performance, reading progress, and your preferences — is stored locally on your device. This data never leaves
 your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

 ## Translation Feature

 The App includes an optional translation feature. When you use this feature, the text of questions and educational content is sent to external translation services to
 generate translations. No personal information is included in these requests. Translated text is cached locally on your device to reduce the need for repeated requests.

 ## Subscriptions and Payments

 The App offers an optional Premium subscription. Purchases are processed by Apple's App Store (on iOS) or Google Play (on Android), which collect and handle payment
 information under their own privacy policies. Subscription state is verified through RevenueCat, a subscription management service that receives an anonymous app-user
 identifier and your purchase events to determine whether your Premium entitlement is active. RevenueCat does not receive your name, email, or payment details.

 ## Analytics and Tracking

 The App does not use advertising, behavioural analytics, or crash reporting services. The only off-device transmissions are the translation requests and anonymous
 subscription events described above.

 ## Data Deletion

 You can delete all locally stored data at any time through the "Clear All Progress" option in Settings. Uninstalling the App will also remove all stored data from your device.

 ## Children's Privacy

 The App does not knowingly collect any information from children. The App is intended for general audiences preparing for the Australian Citizenship Test.
